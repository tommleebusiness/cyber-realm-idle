❓ FAQ

How do I play Cyber Realm Idle?

Simply tap the central button to earn credits. Use credits to buy upgrades that increase your tap power and auto-income. Progress through realms, defeat bosses, and prestige for permanent bonuses!

What is the Prestige system?

Prestige resets your current progress but awards Prestige Points based on your total earned credits. Each point gives +10% permanent tap power, making you stronger on every run!

Is the game free?

Yes! The game is completely free to play. Premium items in the shop are optional and purely cosmetic/boosting. No ads, no pay-to-win!

How do I support the developer?

You can buy gem packs in the shop, purchase the VIP Pass for permanent bonuses, or donate directly via the in-app donation button. Every bit helps! ❤️
